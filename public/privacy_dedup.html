Privacy Policy for DeDup

Last Updated: May 2026

Rahul Pahuja ("we," "us," or "our") operates the DeDup mobile application. Your privacy is important to us, and this document explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users.

  • Account Data: If you log in via Google or Facebook, we collect your name, email, and profile picture to personalize your experience.
  • Device Information: We collect model, OS version, and unique identifiers (via Firebase Analytics) to improve app performance.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect logs when the app crashes to help us fix bugs.

2. Permissions and Data Usage

To provide its core functionality as a storage optimization tool, DeDup requires access to your device's storage. We handle this access with the highest regard for your privacy:

  • All Files Access (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE):
    Why: This is the core permission of the app. It allows us to scan your entire storage (including non-media files) to identify duplicate documents, zip files, and other "junk" that consumes space.
    Usage: We use this to calculate file hashes locally for comparison. We do not read the content of your personal documents for any purpose other than deduplication.
  • Media Permissions (Images, Video, Audio):
    Why: These granular permissions allow us to specifically scan your gallery and music library for duplicate photos, videos, and songs.
    Usage: Used to generate thumbnails for your preview and to perform bit-by-bit comparisons to find exact duplicates.
  • Notifications:
    Why: To alert you when your device storage is running low or when a scanning task is complete.
    Usage: Purely for functional alerts; no marketing notifications are sent without your explicit consent.

Local Processing Guarantee: All file scanning, hashing, and duplicate detection happen entirely on your device. No file names, file contents, or metadata from your storage are ever transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Play Services / Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Auth)
  • Facebook Login SDK

4. Facebook Data Deletion

If you wish to delete data obtained via Facebook Login, you can:

  1. Remove the app from your Facebook Settings (Apps and Websites).
  2. Contact us at the email below to request manual deletion of your account data.

5. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SQLCipher) to protect your local scan history and settings stored on your device.
